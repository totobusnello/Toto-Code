# AI Use Cases & Benchmarks

## Customer Support Automation

### Tier 1 AI Chatbot
- **Reduction**: 40-60% of support tickets handled by AI
- **FTE Impact**: 3-5 agents per 100k tickets/year
- **Tools**: Claude API, Intercom Fin, Zendesk AI, Freshdesk Freddy
- **Implementation**: R$ 150-300k
- **Timeline**: 2-3 months

### Intelligent Ticket Routing
- **Reduction**: 30% faster resolution, 20% fewer escalations
- **FTE Impact**: 0.5-1 supervisor per shift
- **Tools**: Custom ML, Salesforce Einstein
- **Implementation**: R$ 80-150k
- **Timeline**: 1-2 months

### AI Knowledge Base
- **Reduction**: 25% self-service increase

- **Implementation**: R$ 50-100k
