## Anti-Patterns to Avoid

❌ **Generic hero images** - Stock photos of people pointing at screens
❌ **Feature overload** - Listing 20+ features on homepage
❌ **Unclear value prop** - "Revolutionize your workflow" says nothing
❌ **Carousel dependency** - Auto-playing sliders with key information
❌ **Hidden pricing** - Forces demos for basic pricing info
❌ **Modal bombardment** - Pop-ups immediately on page load
❌ **Slow loading** - Heavy animations blocking content
❌ **Cookie-cutter templates** - Generic SaaS template look

2. **AI Feature Highlights** - Dedicated AI capability sections
3. **Interactive Demos** - Embedded product sandboxes
4. **Dark Mode Default** - Especially for technical products
5. **Variable Fonts** - Animated typography
6. **3D Elements** - Subtle depth and dimension
7. **Glassmorphism** - Frosted glass effects
8. **Grain Textures** - Adding warmth and depth
